Tipos de Agentes:
- carAg(ap.bumpAg, ap.stoplightAg... )
	-Setup
		-Velocidad= 0
		-Rendimiento= 100%
		-Passengers >= 1
		-startpos
		-endpos
	-speed up
	-slow down
- bumpAg() -static
- stoplightAg()
	-Setup
		-state=randomState
	-changeState
- signAg -static
- personAg
	-Setup
		-Movement
	-changeMovement
	-startpos
	-endpos
- weatherAg
	-Setup
		-Random(Sunny, Rainy, Foggy)
	-changeWeather
-roadCondAg(ap.weatherAg)
	-Setup
		-Random(0-100%)
	-weatherImpact
-parkingSportAg
	-Setup
		-randomOccupancy(0-100%)
		-queue
	-UpdateQueues
